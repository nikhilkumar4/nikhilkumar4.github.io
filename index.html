About Me

Before joining TIFR, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Combinatorics and Optimization Group at the University of Waterloo and Algorithm Engineering Group at the Hasso Plattner Institute (HPI). I completed my B.Tech and M.Tech degrees in Computer Science and Engineering at IIT Delhi in 2014, and earned my Ph.D. there in 2020 under the supervision of Prof. Naveen Garg.

My research interests are in Theoretical Computer Science, with a focus on Combinatorial Optimization and Approximation Algorithms.
