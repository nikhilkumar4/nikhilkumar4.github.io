Approximation Algorithms

Vigyan Vidushi–2026 | TIFR Mumbai

Lecturer: Nikhil Kumar

Course Overview

This short course introduces the design and analysis of approximation algorithms for NP-hard optimization problems. The course focuses on fundamental techniques through classical problems in combinatorial optimization.

Topics Covered

  • Lecture 1: Introduction to Approximation Algorithms
  • Lecture 2: Matchings and the Vertex Cover Problem
  • Lecture 3: Steiner Tree Problem
  • Lecture 4: Metric Traveling Salesman Problem (Metric TSP)

References

  1. Vijay V. Vazirani, Approximation Algorithms.
  2. David P. Williamson and David B. Shmoys, The Design of Approximation Algorithms.
